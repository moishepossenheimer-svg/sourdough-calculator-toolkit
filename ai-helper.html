Sourdough helper

A lightweight rule-based helper for common questions. It is best used for quick direction while baking.

What this helper is good for

It is useful for common patterns: hydration, starter percentage, feed ratios, salt, weak starter and fermentation signs. For food safety, mould, unusual smells or illness, use conservative judgement and reliable food safety guidance.
