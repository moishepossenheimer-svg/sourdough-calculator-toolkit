About Sourdough Toolkit

Sourdough Toolkit exists for the moment when a recipe says watch the dough and you are not completely sure what you are watching for.

The site combines small calculators with practical explanations. The calculators handle the arithmetic: hydration, starter feeds, baker percentage, recipe scaling and bulk fermentation windows. The guides explain how those numbers show up in the bowl, on the bench and in the baked loaf.

Editorial approach

The guidance here is intentionally conservative. It favours repeatable home baking over dramatic extremes, because most people improve faster with dough they can shape than with a very wet dough they cannot control yet.

Use the calculators as planning tools, then use your dough as the final source of truth. Flour strength, room temperature, starter health and handling all matter.
