How Much Starter Should I Use?

Starter percentage affects how quickly dough ferments. More starter usually means faster fermentation.

FlourStarterStarter %Use
500g50g10%Slower rise
500g100g20%Balanced
500g150g30%Faster rise

Warm rooms and cold rooms

Use less starter in warm rooms and more starter in cooler rooms. For overnight dough, a lower starter percentage can help stop overproofing.
