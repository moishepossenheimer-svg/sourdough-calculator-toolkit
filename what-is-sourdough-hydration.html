What Is Sourdough Hydration?

Sourdough hydration means the amount of water compared with the flour weight. If a dough has 500g flour and 350g water, the hydration is 70%.

Common examples

FlourWaterHydrationFeel
500g325g65%Easier
500g350g70%Good beginner level
500g400g80%Sticky and advanced

Best hydration for beginners

Many beginners do best around 65–70%. It is wet enough for a good loaf but easier to shape than very wet dough.

Use the Dough Calculator to test different hydrations.
