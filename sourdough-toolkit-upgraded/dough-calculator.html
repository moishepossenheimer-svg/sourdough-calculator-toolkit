Calculator

Sourdough dough calculator

Enter your flour amount and preferred baker percentages. The calculator gives water, starter, salt and total dough weight in grams.

Practical note

For most beginner loaves, 65-72% hydration, 15-25% starter and 2% salt is a dependable range. Keep the starter assumption in mind: this calculator treats the starter as equal parts flour and water.
