Sourdough Bulk Fermentation Times Chart

Bulk fermentation depends on temperature, starter strength and dough condition.

Room tempRough bulk time
18°C7–10 hours
20°C5.5–8 hours
22°C4–6 hours
24°C3–5 hours

Signs it is ready

The dough should look puffier, feel airy, have bubbles around the edges and jiggle slightly.
