Sourdough Feeding Ratios Explained

Feeding ratios are written as starter : flour : water.

1:1:1

20g starter + 20g flour + 20g water.

1:2:2

20g starter + 40g flour + 40g water.

1:5:5

10g starter + 50g flour + 50g water. This lasts longer before peaking.

Use the Starter Feeding Calculator for exact amounts.
